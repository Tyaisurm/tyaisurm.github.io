Custom CSV Export

About

Customizable CSV data export Add-On for Google Forms! 'Custom CSV Export' allows you to easily modify the formatting when exporting raw data out from Google Forms surveys as CSV files.

Link to G Suite Marketplace to be added soon!

Terms of Use

'Custom CSV Export' G Suite add-on was created to be used as an alternative tool to the default CSV export functionality already provided in Google Forms. If you find this tool useful, then that's enough for me :)

Privacy Policy

'Custom CSV Export' add-on does not collect, log, or store any personal information. 'Custom CSV Export' add-on requires access to your Google Drive's folders and files in order to create new files and folders, and to check that no dublicate files or folders are generated during export.
